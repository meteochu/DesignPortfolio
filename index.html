      Axla 3

      Hey. I'm back.


      Available for iOS 9



      Axla 3 is the next level theme that refines everything its predecessors weren't. The colour pallete was hand picked and remade, and many of the glyphs in the icons were scraped and redrawn from scratch. Axla 3 is the next generation theme that further connects the colourfulness and simplicity of the previous versions.

      Axla 3 includes over 400 redesigned app icons, 300 over Axla 1.0 and 200 over Axla 2.0! It supports all devices running iOS 9; from the iPad 2 to the iPad Air 2; iPod touch (5th gen and 6th gen); and iPhone 4s to iPhone 6s / 6s Plus.
      Apply with Winterboard OR Anemone!

      Icon Request Instructions

      1. Find the .IPA File of the app you'd like to request and unzip the file.
      2. Navigate to /AppName/Payload/AppName.app/ and look for the Info.plist file and copy it
      3. Look for the actaul Icon Files. AppIcon..., Icon..., app_icon...
      4. Copy the icon files and the Info.plist file, and add it to a folder and compress it into a .zip file
      5. Email me at andy@meteochu.me with the Zip file as the attachment and specify what app it is.
      6. Wait for the next update :)
